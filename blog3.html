How to Read Nutrition Labels Like a Pro

By EatWell | April 10, 2025

Ever feel overwhelmed staring at a nutrition label? You're not alone. Here's a breakdown of how to read one and make smarter food choices:

  • Serving Size: Always check this first! All the information below is based on that size.
  • Calories: Gives a quick overview of how much energy you'll get from one serving.
  • Macronutrients: Pay attention to fats, proteins, and carbohydrates.
  • Ingredients: Less is more—fewer ingredients usually means fewer additives.

Mastering labels helps you take control of your health. Next time you're at the store, flip the box and scan with confidence!
