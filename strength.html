Strength Exercises

A classic bodyweight exercise to build upper body strength and endurance.

Squats

Targets your lower body, improving strength and stability in the legs and glutes.

Deadlifts

Full-body strength movement focusing on your back, legs, and core.

Strengthens the biceps and forearms, improving arm strength and muscle tone.

Great for upper body strength, especially targeting the back and arms.
