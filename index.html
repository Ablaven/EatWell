Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is EatWell?
EatWell is a personalized food organizer that helps you manage your meals based on your health goals, preferences, and conditions. Whether you're looking to lose weight, manage diabetes, or just eat healthier, we provide meal plans tailored to you.
How do I get started?
Simply choose the health plan that suits your goals, and we'll help you get started with personalized meal suggestions. You can sign up or log in to customize your preferences.
Can I change my plan later?
Yes! You can modify your meal plan anytime. Simply log into your account and update your preferences as needed.
Do you provide a shopping list for ingredients?
Yes! We provide a shopping list for each meal plan to make it easier for you to purchase the necessary ingredients for your meals.
How do I report a problem?
If you face any issues, you can report them directly from the "Report A Problem" section on our website. We'll address your concerns as quickly as possible.
