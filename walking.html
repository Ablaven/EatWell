Walking Exercises

Brisk Walking

A fast-paced walk that raises your heart rate and improves endurance.

Incline Walking

Walking uphill or on an inclined treadmill helps tone the legs and boost cardio fitness.

Interval Walking

Alternate between slow and fast walking to increase calorie burn and stamina.

Walking Lunges

Combines walking and lunges to build lower body strength and flexibility.

Walk & Talk

Perfect for light exercise while socializing, helping you stay active throughout the day.
