Welcome to EatWell – User Guide

This guide will help you understand how to use EatWell’s features to support your healthy lifestyle goals.

Browsing and Using Meal Plans

EatWell offers personalized meal plans tailored to specific health conditions and fitness goals.

  • Scroll to the “Get Your Plan” section on the homepage.
  • Select the category that suits your needs (e.g., Diabetes, Hypertension, Weight Loss).
  • Each plan includes a daily meal schedule, recommended food items, and dietary tips.
  • Click on any plan to read full details, including do’s and don’ts.
  • Follow the sample meals and nutritional notes to stay on track.

Exploring and Following Exercise Routines

Stay active with a variety of structured exercise routines grouped by type and goal.

  • Scroll to the “Get Moving with EatWell Fitness” section on the homepage.
  • Select a category: Strength, Cardio, Yoga, Pilates, HIIT, Dance, Walking, or Cycling.
  • Each category includes several routines with step-by-step guidance.
  • Click on any exercise to view detailed instructions, duration, and recommended frequency.
  • Choose the routines that match your ability level and schedule.

Consulting with Doctors

For medical support, you can connect with certified doctors directly on the platform.

  • Go to the “Doctors” section in the main menu.
  • Browse through the list of specialists based on your needs.
  • Click on a doctor’s profile to view their bio, specialty, contact information, and availability.
  • Use the provided email or phone number to reach out.
  • Some doctors may offer direct messaging options through their profile pages.

Shopping on the EatWell Store

Purchase healthy food products and wellness items from our curated online store.

  • Select “Shop” from the main menu.
  • Browse by product category: Snacks, Supplements, Organic Foods, Beverages, etc.
  • Click on a product to see benefits and pricing in EGP.
  • Add items to your cart and proceed to checkout securely.
  • Your cart includes a gift progress circle — fill it to 100% by spending over EGP 650 to unlock a free reward.
