Simple Home Workouts + What to Eat Before and After

By EatWell | April 5, 2025

🏠 Quick Home Workouts

  • 10 squats + 10 lunges
  • 15 push-ups + 30-second plank
  • 20 jumping jacks + repeat 3x

🍎 What to Eat

Before:

A light carb like a banana or a slice of whole grain bread. It gives you energy without weighing you down.

After:

Focus on protein and carbs to help your muscles recover—try Greek yogurt with berries, or scrambled eggs with toast.

Working out at home can be effective and energizing when paired with the right nutrition. You’ve got this!
