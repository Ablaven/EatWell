The Truth About Sugar: How Much Is Too Much?

By EatWell | April 8, 2025

Sugar is everywhere, and it’s not always easy to know how much is too much. The American Heart Association recommends:

  • 🔸 No more than 25g/day for women
  • 🔸 No more than 36g/day for men

Too much sugar can increase your risk of obesity, diabetes, and heart disease. Try these tips to cut back:

  • Swap soda for sparkling water with fruit.
  • Read labels to spot hidden sugars (look for words ending in “-ose”).
  • Eat whole fruits instead of sweetened snacks.

Balancing sugar intake doesn’t mean cutting it completely. It’s all about awareness and moderation!
